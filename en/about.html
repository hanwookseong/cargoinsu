About N2N Insurance Brokerage

So that cargo owners can
compare and choose insurers.

cargoinsu.com is operated by N2N Insurance Brokerage. When requesting a marine cargo quote, a shipper usually hears back from a single insurer. We work differently — one application reviews acceptance across six insurers (AIG, Chubb, DB Insurance, KB Insurance, Meritz Fire, Hyundai Marine) at the same time.

Why a solo broker is an advantage

01

Independence

No insurer-specific sales pressure — we review the insurer that fits the shipment. Large agencies carry insurer quotas and incentives; a licensed broker acts on behalf of the cargo owner under Article 89 of the Insurance Business Act.

02

Fast response

We reply within 1–2 business days as a rule. With no layered sign-off of a large organisation, a quote request is handled right away.

How our fee works

Our brokerage fee is paid by the insurer. It is never added to the premium the cargo owner pays.

This structure complies with Article 98 of the Insurance Business Act (prohibition of special benefits), and is what lets us compare six insurers for the shipper at no extra cost.
